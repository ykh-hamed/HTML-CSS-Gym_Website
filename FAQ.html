Frequently Asked Questions :

How do I join?

It’s a really simple process – and it’s all handled here on the website. Click on join now from the main menu, choose the class you want to join, select your type of membership, fill in your information and you can pay later when you come yo the gym. You’ll be a member in just a few simple steps.

Can I try the gym before joining?

Yes, you can visit us have a look at the equipment check personal trainers performance in the gym but to try a class you have to pay 20 L.E.

What happens if I have problems while I’m trying to join?

If you have a problem while filling or submitting the form in join now page you can come later to the gym and fill in the form manually or you can simply inform us about the problem via email attached in contact us page and we will be in touch with you as soon as possible.

What is the minimum age to join The Gym?

You need to be 18 to join Body Building classes otherwise joining any of the other three classes you need to be 12 years old.

Can I cancel my membership?

yes,you’ve got complete freedom to cancel at any time, with no penalties at all.

What is the gym working days and hours?

The gym is opened 7 days a week 24 hours a day.

Are there lockers and showers in The Gym?

Yes, we provide showers, lockers and toilets.

What is the time schedule between men and women?

Women time starts from 10am till 5pm and men starts from 5pm till next women time.
